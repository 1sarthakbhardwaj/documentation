<Note>
The objective of this tutorial is to help New and potential clients/users get started with labellerr. The tutorial covers all login procedures for convenience in the case of any issues faced by a new user with the signup process.
</Note>

## Labellerr Signup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accessing The Platform">
    Once on our webpage, on the home screen, a floating toolbar with a few options is visible on the top. Click on the **Login Button**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/labellerr-cdn/1%20%20Documentation/homepage.webp" alt="Labellerr Homepage" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Up The Credentials">
    This would redirect you to the Login/Sign Up screen. Here, you can choose either to click on the **Sign Up** option or the **Continue with Google** option.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/labellerr-cdn/1%20%20Documentation/sign-up-email.webp" alt="Sign up screen" />
    </Frame>

    <Info>
    **If the 'Sign Up' option is selected:**
    - A confirmation email would be sent to the registered email address
    - Be sure to check the Socials/Promotion page if the email is not visible on your Primary list
    - Click on the Verification link to proceed

    **If the 'Continue with Google' option is selected:**
    - Choose one of the active emails to proceed
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Domain Creation">
    Give your account a domain name that suits your preference. In case the suggested name is not accepted, try using a different name.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/labellerr-cdn/1%20%20Documentation/domain-creation.webp" alt="Domain creation screen" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Project Creation and Dashboard">
    Once domain is created, you would be redirected to create project page. Give a suitable name to your project and you can get started right away!

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/labellerr-cdn/1%20%20Documentation/project-creation.webp" alt="Project creation screen" />
    </Frame>

    For more, check out the video tutorial.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
For further assistance contact **support@tensormatics.com**
</Note>
